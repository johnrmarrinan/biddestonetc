Membership

Biddestone Tennis Club has excellent facilities of four courts, two of which are floodlit for year round play.The cost of membership is relatively low and new members of all standard are most welcome.

Coaching

Junior Coaching is available on Saturday afternoons from Spring through to Autumn with our LTA qualified coach. Adult coaching is also available for individual or groups.

Social Tennis

Sunday Club Mornings are held on the first Sunday of every month - just turn up and play, non members welcome! Mixed Doubles, Ladies and Men's Tournaments are held during the Summer. The Club runs seven league teams for those who want more competitive tennis.

Junior Coaching runs on Saturday afternoons from 1 until 5pm. Juniors have to be members of the club to join a coaching class. The bookable court is available to others whilst junior coaching is in progress. Contact Lynne Gill (see below) to enquire about junior coaching.

Members can organise individual or group coaching by emailing Roger Hurley (Roger.Hurley@hotmail.com) for men's coaching or Lynne Gill (any14tennis@hotmail.com) for ladies' and junior coaching.

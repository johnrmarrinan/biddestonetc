The club runs a number of league teams in both summer and winter leagues so we aim to accommodate all members wanting to play league tennis. When league matches are being played they take two courts. Note only one league match is scheduled per weekday (Monday through Thursday) so apart from occasional rearranged league matches (which should be booked by the Match Captain!) we always maintain 1 non bookable court.

Note Winter League matches reserve the two floodlit courts on Monday evenings.

The club runs a number of league teams in both summer and winter leagues so we aim to accommodate all members wanting to play league tennis.

In the summer of 2015 Biddestone Tennis Club entered seven teams in the Chippenham and District Tennis League.

Fixtures lists and contacts are available from the Biddestone team captains.

If you are interested in playing please contact the captains directly or email the club info@biddestonetc

Score cards and rules can be found on the league web site:Chippenham and District Tennis League
